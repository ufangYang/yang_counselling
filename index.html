About me.

I hold a PhD in Psychology from the University of Bristol and have lived and worked in Taiwan, the UK, France, Belgium, and Germany. This multicultural journey shapes everything I do. Based in Berlin, I specialize in helping professionals with multicultural backgrounds navigate the emotional and behavioral challenges of living across environments.

With a background in cognitive neuroscience, my approach blends evidence-based CBT with insights from how the brain learns, adapts, and heals. Combined with intercultural awareness, this creates a space where your cultural background, your neurobiology, and your current reality are all honoured.

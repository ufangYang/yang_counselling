About me.

I hold a PhD in Psychology from the University of Bristol and have lived and worked in Taiwan, the UK, France, Belgium, and Germany. This multicultural journey shapes everything I do. Based in Berlin, I specialize in helping expats and international professionals navigate the emotional and behavioral challenges of living in new environments.

My approach blends evidence-based CBT with intercultural awareness, creating a space where your cultural background and current reality are both honoured.

CBT gives you practical, evidence-based tools to identify and change unhelpful thought patterns. By combining it with Intercultural Mindfulness, we also honour the cultural context that shapes your experience, creating an approach that addresses both your inner world and the realities of living across cultures.

Sessions are 50 minutes, conducted via secure video. Online counselling offers the same depth as in-person work, with the flexibility of connecting from wherever you are. All you need is a private, quiet space and a stable connection.

My practice is designed for expats, international professionals, students, and anyone navigating life across cultures. Whether you\u2019ve just arrived in a new country or have been abroad for years, if you\u2019re dealing with burnout, career uncertainty, attachment challenges, or a sense of disconnection, this space is for you.
